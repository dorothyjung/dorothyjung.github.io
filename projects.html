Brainability

Mobile application to provide neurofeedback on levels of personal brain activity. In combination with thought exercises based on research in cognitive psychology, Brainability helps the user balance the brain activity between their hemispheres and avoids the types of repetitive brain pattern which lead to headaches, anxiety, and lack of focus. Won the Judges' Choice award at a hackathon sponsored by Emotiv/Pivotal Labs. Hosted here.
